About

This site hosts freely available clinical prediction tools developed and validated by the Clinical Abdominal Surgery Helsinki group, led by adjunct professor Ville Sallinen MD PhD.

Our Research Group

We are a surgical research group based in Helsinki, Finland, focused on randomized controlled trial, clinical prediction modeling and surgical outcomes in hepatopancreatobiliary and general gastrointestinal surgery. Our work aims to improve patient outcomes through data-driven tools and validated clinical prediction models.

Learn more about our publications and ongoing work on ResearchGate (Ville Sallinen) . @villesallinen
